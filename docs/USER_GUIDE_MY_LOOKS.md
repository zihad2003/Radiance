## ğŸ”’ Privacy & Storage

### Where is my data stored?
- **On your device only!** All looks are stored in your browser's local storage
- **No cloud uploads:** Your images never leave your computer
- **No tracking:** We don't collect or analyze your saved looks

### How much storage do I have?
- You can save **100-2000 looks** depending on your browser
- Each look takes about **500 KB** of space
- Your browser will warn you if you're running out of space

### How do I clear all my looks?
1. Open the "My Looks" gallery
2. Scroll to the bottom
3. Click **"Clear All"** in the footer
4. Confirm the action
5. All looks will be permanently deleted

## â“ Troubleshooting

### "Failed to save look" error
**Possible causes:**
- Browser storage is full
- Browser doesn't support IndexedDB
- Camera is not active

**Solutions:**
- Delete old looks to free up space
- Update your browser to the latest version
- Make sure the camera is turned on

### Looks not appearing in gallery
**Possible causes:**
- Browser cache issue
- IndexedDB corruption

**Solutions:**
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache (Settings â†’ Clear browsing data)
- Try a different browser

### Export button not working
**Possible causes:**
- Browser popup blocker
- Download restrictions

**Solutions:**
- Allow popups for Radiance
- Check browser download settings
- Try right-clicking the image and "Save As"
